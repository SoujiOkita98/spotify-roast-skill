🎧 Spotify Playlist Roast

Extract any public playlist → JSON. Then let an AI judge your taste.

What it does

A lightweight tool to pull every track from a public Spotify playlist into clean JSON — no OAuth, no app registration, no client secret.

Then a recipe to turn that JSON into a doodle-style "AI verdict" page that's screenshot-friendly, exports to PNG, and toggles EN / 中.

What you can do with it

  • 🔥 Roast your playlist into a shareable doodle page (see the demo)
  • 🤖 Hand the JSON to your AI so it actually knows your music taste
  • 🎬 Ask AI to pick songs from your real library for a vlog / mood
  • 🔍 Audit duplicates, count languages, see decade distribution

Try the demo

The "Life sucks" example: a 168-track playlist named after sadness, secretly stuffed with Three Little Birds and What A Wonderful World. The verdict was unkind.
